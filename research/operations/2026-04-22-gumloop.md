### Coverage gaps (built from role knowledge, not Gumloop)

Gumloop's ops catalog is thin on the unglamorous but essential ops work. The following aren't on Gumloop but a real solo-founder operator needs them:

1. **The shared `company-operating-context.md`** — the operating doc (company map, rituals, key contacts, hard nos, active priorities) that the Vendor agent reads before it acts. This is the single highest-leverage pattern in the workspace and it has no Gumloop equivalent. Owned by `head-of-operations` via `define-operating-context`.
2. **Weekly cross-agent review (`run-weekly-review`)** — the Monday rhythm that reads every agent's `outputs.json` and produces the "what happened across my ops" readout. Inferred from `founder-marketing-workspace`'s `review-marketing-health` pattern, not Gumloop.
3. **Renewal calendar (`track-renewals`)** — nobody ships a template for "extract my renewal dates from a contract folder and build a calendar with lead-time flags." But it's the single highest-value vendor-ops output for a solo founder.
4. **SaaS spend audit (`audit-saas-spend`)** — scans connected billing + inbox + contract library for the subscriptions the founder forgot, flags duplicates, computes annualized spend, surfaces the 3 cancel candidates. Gumloop has nothing close.
5. **Vendor-outreach drafting (`draft-vendor-outreach`)** — negotiation / cancel / trial-request drafts. Derivative of the generic `draft-reply` pattern but domain-specific enough to deserve its own skill on the vendor agent.

**Net: ~5 of 15 work skills are coverage-gap-driven.** The Gumloop catalog validated 10 skills; role knowledge supplied the other 5.
